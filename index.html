äbout me

My name is Robin Frischmann and I am a software engineer based in Karlsruhe, Germany. In September 2016 I finished my B.Sc. in computer science.

I focus on web & mobile app development and love creating beautiful solutions of all kind. I truly believe in open source and love to pay back to the community. Therefore, most of my work is on Github. I use JavaScript, React, Redux, Fela, GraphQL and Elm amongst others and am interested in functional programming of all kind.

I speak German, English, Norwegian, Swedish and some French as well.
If not coding you will most likely catch me outdoors running, exploring and travelling our nature. I love design, architecture, language, (playing) music, vegan food and well-written books as well as yoga and meditation.
